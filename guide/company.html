            # Company information

            Field Description
            Name Enter company name. (required)
            Address Enter company address. (required)
            City Enter city. (required)
            State/Prov Enter state or province.
            Zip/Post Code Enter zip/postal code.
            Country Select the country. (required)
            Tel Enter telephone number. (required)
            Fax Enter facsimile number.
            Website Enter company website if available.
            Email Enter company email. (required)
            Contact Name Enter the name of contact person.
            Contact Position Enter the position of contact person.
            Category Select the primary category of business.
            Incorporated Year Enter the year since the business started.
            Introduction Enter a brief introduction of the company.
            Keywords Enter keywords for directory search.
            Display Toggle to show or hide on directory.
            Exporter Toggle on if you export.
            Importer Toggle on if you import.
            Export Products Enter your export products.
            Current Market Enter the countries your company is exporting to.
            Target Market Enter the countries your company likes to expand next.
            Relation Sought Enter the type of business relationship your company seeks.
            Certifications Enter the certifications your company has received.
            Remark Enter additional remarks about your company if applicable.
            Social Ids Enter your company's social ids if applicable.
