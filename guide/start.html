            # Getting Started

            The fastest way is to set up a free Basic membership account. Follow the simple steps below to start using ExportRim immediately.

            # Create an account

            Click the Sign Up button on the upper right corner. Enter a unique username, email, and password. If the username is already being used, the system will request you to try another username. Make sure you remember your username because this is required to confirm your registration and to retrieve a forgotten password. Your email address cannot be used to retrieve a forgotten password.

            # Confirm and sign in

            When your signup credential is accepted, the system will email you a confirmation code. Check your email and enter the confirmation code. Once you have confirmed successfully, you can sign in with your username and password.

            # Manage company

            The first thing to do is to set up your company profile. Refer to the section Company Profile - Add new company. You can add your catalog items, news blog, and other information whenever you like.
