            To manage an online catalog for your company.

            # Add new item

            Click the on the top right.

            # Edit item

            Click the icon.

            # Delete item

            Click the icon.

            # Item information

            Field Description
            Name Enter item name. (required)
            Code Enter item code.
            Category Select the category for this item. (required)
            Description Enter the item description. (required)
            Specifications Enter the item's detail specifications.
            Certifications Enter the certifications available for this item.
            Minimum Order Quantity Enter the minimum order quantity.
            Price Range Enter price range.
            Example: US$1-5/pc
            Lead Time Enter lead time.
            Example: 4-6 weeks
            Incoterms Enter your company's incoterms.
            Footnote Enter additional remarks.
            Example: Private label available.
            Packing Details
            Quantity Enter the quantity per packing.
            Gross Weight (kg) Enter the gross weight (kg) per packing.
            Net Weight (kg) Enter the net weight (kg) per packing.
            Length (cm) Enter the length (cm) packing dimension for shipment.
            Width (cm) Enter the width (cm) packing dimension for shipment .
            Height (cm) Enter the height (cm) packing dimension for shipment.
            Skid Details
            Quantity Enter the total quantity per skid.
            Gross Weight (kg) Enter the gross weight (kg) per skid.
            Net Weight (kg) Enter the gross weight (kg) per skid.
            Length (cm) Enter the length (cm) skid dimension for shipment..
            Width (cm) Enter the width (cm) skid dimension for shipment.
            Height (cm) Enter the height (cm) skid dimension for shipment.
            Container Load
            20ft Std Enter the total quantity per 20ft container load.
            40ft Std Enter the total quantity per 40ft container load .
            40ft HC Enter the total quantity per 40ft hi-cube container load.
            Photo Upload item photo from your computer.
            Display Toggle to show or hide the item.
            Ranking Enter display position for item. Rank from high to low.
